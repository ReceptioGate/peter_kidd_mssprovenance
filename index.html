Peter Kidd: A Documented Case of Defamation and Manuscript Dismemberment

This page presents documented information about Peter Kidd, author of the blog mssprovenance, and his role in promoting the dismemberment of medieval manuscripts through auction houses, blogs, and private networks.

Background

In 2022, Prof. Carla Rossi submitted an official report to the Italian Carabinieri (TPC) denouncing a network of 26 art dealers involved in the illegal trade and dismemberment of medieval manuscripts. Following this, she became the target of a sustained smear campaign, including false obituaries (example here), threats, and defamatory claims—many of which were disseminated or endorsed by Peter Kidd.
